Services

What I can help with.

Business Process Optimisation
If something in your business takes longer than it should, involves more steps than it needs to, or keeps breaking down in the same place, that's worth looking at. I map out how work actually flows, find the problems, and help you sort them out practically — without unnecessary complexity or a six-month change programme. I've done this in contact centres, government-contracted programmes, and compliance-heavy operational environments across Australia and New Zealand.
Customer Experience Consulting
Most CX problems aren't really about the people — they're about how the work is set up. I've spent years inside contact centres and customer operations, including programmes delivered on behalf of the Australian Commonwealth Department of Health and Aged Care and New South Wales State Government electoral services. I know where things tend to go wrong and how to fix them in a way that actually sticks.
Web & App Development
Clean, functional digital products for small and medium-sized businesses. Whether you need a new website, a web application, or a mobile app, I build things that are straightforward to use and built to last. No bloat, no unnecessary complexity.

About

I've spent the better part of a decade working across customer experience, operations, and compliance — contact centres, retail, healthcare support, hospitality — in New Zealand and Australia. Different industries, but a lot of the same problems.

That work has taken me into some complex environments: operations delivered on behalf of the Australian Commonwealth Department of Health and Aged Care, New South Wales State Government electoral services, and large-scale contact centres where compliance, quality, and consistency aren't optional. I hold an RG146 Tier 1 Superannuation certification and a BA in Political Science from the University of Waikato.

These days I also run Parallax Labs, an AI software studio based in Auckland. The consulting is a natural extension of that — I like working directly with businesses on the stuff that's frustrating them, not just building software about it.

If something isn't working the way it should, I'm usually pretty good at figuring out why.
