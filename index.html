Services

What I can help with.

Customer Service Consulting
Most customer service issues aren't really about the people. They're about how the work is set up. I've spent years inside contact centres and customer operations, so I know where things tend to go wrong and how to fix them without a six-month change programme.
Business Process Optimisation
If something in your business takes longer than it should, involves more steps than it needs to, or keeps breaking down in the same place, that's worth looking at. I map out how work actually flows, find the problems, and help you sort them out practically.

About

I've spent the better part of a decade working across customer experience, operations, and compliance in New Zealand and Australia: contact centres, retail, healthcare support, hospitality. Different industries, but a lot of the same problems.

These days I run Parallax Labs, an AI software studio based in Auckland. The consulting is a natural extension of that - I like working directly with businesses on the stuff that's frustrating them, not just building software about it.

If something isn't working the way it should, I'm usually pretty good at figuring out why.
